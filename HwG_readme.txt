Description: Play as a baby seal and journey through a narrow and perilous passageway to reach your family on the other side. Hide from and outmaneuver an ancient and battle-scarred orca while also maintaining enough oxygen to make it through to the end.

Instructions on how to play:

PC: WASD to move around on land, steer with the mouse and press w to move around underwater. Keep track of air, refilling at airholes when necessary, and make it to the macguffin while avoiding the orca by hiding in the kelp and sea grass. Press P in Gameplay Prototype for Pause Menu.

Xbox: Use the left joystick to move and the right joystick to look around. Press down on the left joystick to sprint. Use the A button to jump and select menu options. Use the options button to open the pause menu.
